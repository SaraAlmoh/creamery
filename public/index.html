About Us

Scoops n' Smiles is one of the world's largest chain of ice cream specialty shops, providing guests with a wide array of ice cream flavors and delicious treats at more than 500 retail shops in more than 12 countries around the world. The brand was founded by two ice cream enthusiasts whose passion for ice cream led to the creation of many iconic ice cream flavors including Pralines 'n Cream, Jamoca Almond Fudge and Very Berry Strawberry. Today, Scoops n' Smiles has more than 1,300 ice creams in its flavor library, and also offers custom ice cream cakes, frozen beverages and the Polar Pizza Ice Cream Treat. Its franchised ice cream shops serve as places where people can connect and create special memories while they explore a wide array of flavors, including a new Flavor of the Month every month. Headquartered in Canton, Mass., Scoops n' Smiles is part of the Dunkin' Brands Group, Inc. (Nasdaq: DNKN) family of companies.
